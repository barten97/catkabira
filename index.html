Meet Cat Kabira

I've been teaching yoga, meditation, energy work, and personal empowerment since 2002. What got me into these practices was my own struggle with anorexia and bulimia and healing from childhood trauma and abuse.

I never thought that I would heal or be able to function in this world. Most days I didn't want to be here on this planet and yet when I discovered subtle energy in my body, a whole new universe opened for me and suddenly the whole world made sense.  
 
This is why my message to you is - "It's worth being here!"
